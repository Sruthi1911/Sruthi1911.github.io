Ingredients-index:

Biotique products generally feature a blend of Ayurvedic ingredients and natural botanical extracts, often including ingredients like neem, ashwagandha, wild turmeric, and various oils like almond, olive, and coconut oil, aiming to be chemical-free and suitable for all skin types.

Here's a more detailed look at the ingredients commonly used in Biotique products:

Neem:

Known for its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties, often used in products for skin and hair care.

A popular herb in Ayurveda, believed to have rejuvenating and stress-reducing properties.

Biotique's Approach to Ingredients:

100% Natural Botanical Extracts:

Biotique emphasizes the use of natural ingredients derived from plants and Ayurveda.

Chemical-Free:

They claim to be free from harmful chemicals like parabens, sulfates, and synthetic fragrances.

Dermatologically Tested:

Many Biotique products are dermatologically tested to ensure safety and suitability for various skin types.

Cruelty-Free:

Biotique products are not tested on animals.
